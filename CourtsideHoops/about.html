About Courtside Hoops

Passion for the game, dedication to collectors

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Courtside Hoops connects basketball fans with the cards they love. What started as a small collector group is now a trusted NBA trading platform.

Every card tells a story — rookie seasons, championship moments, and legendary performances. We help preserve those moments through collecting.
