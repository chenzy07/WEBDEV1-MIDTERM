Welcome to Luna Vista Resort

Where Modern Luxury Meets the Ocean's Edge

Founded with the mission to combine tradition with a touch of the unexpected, Luna Vista Resort offers a sanctuary for those seeking a "home away from home". Our property blends minimalist contemporary design with the natural beauty of our tropical surroundings.

Resort Highlights

Beachfront Paradise

Enjoy direct access to pristine white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters just steps away from your room.

Infinity Pool

Relax in our luxurious infinity pool overlooking the ocean, perfect for sunset views and evening swims.

Luxury Rooms

Spacious rooms with modern interiors, comfortable beds, and breathtaking sea views.

Island Dining

Experience local Filipino cuisine and international favorites prepared by our world-class chefs.

Spa & Wellness

Rejuvenate your body and mind with relaxing spa treatments and wellness experiences.

Sunset Views

Witness unforgettable sunsets from our beachfront lounge and rooftop viewing areas.
